Friendicoes, New Delhi

Friendicoes, located in New Delhi, is a renowned animal welfare organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming stray and abandoned animals. With a team of passionate volunteers and professionals, Friendicoes provides essential veterinary care, shelter, and advocacy for the welfare of animals in need.

Charlie’s Animal Rescue Centre, Bengaluru

Charlie’s Animal Rescue Centre (CARE), based in Bengaluru, is committed to rescuing, sheltering, and rehoming animals in need. Through compassionate care and community outreach, CARE strives to create a safer and more compassionate environment for animals across Bengaluru, promoting adoption, sterilization, and responsible pet ownership.

BSPCA, Mumbai

The Bombay Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (BSPCA), located in Mumbai, is dedicated to the welfare and protection of animals in the city. With a focus on rescue, rehabilitation, and advocacy, BSPCA works tirelessly to ensure the well-being of animals through medical treatment, adoption services, and public awareness campaigns, striving to create a more compassionate society for both animals and humans alike.

Red Paws Rescue

Red Paws Rescue is a Delhi-based organization committed to rescuing and rehabilitating stray and abandoned animals. Through their dedicated team of volunteers and foster network, Red Paws Rescue provides veterinary care, shelter, and adoption services, working tirelessly to improve the lives of animals in need and promote responsible pet ownership in the community.

Stray Relief and Animal Welfare (STRAW) India

Stray Relief and Animal Welfare (STRAW) India is a non-profit organization based in New Delhi, dedicated to promoting the welfare of stray animals. Through rescue, sterilization, vaccination, and adoption programs, STRAW India works to alleviate the suffering of stray animals and create a more compassionate society. They also focus on community education and advocacy to raise awareness about the importance of animal welfare and responsible pet ownership.
