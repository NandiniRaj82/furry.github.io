Pet Health

Choose the Right Food for Your Dog:

  • Puppy Food:(0-1 year)
  • Royal Canin Puppy Food: Their puppy formulas are tailored to different breeds, sizes, and specific needs, providing the necessary nutrients for healthy growth.
  • Blue Buffalo Life Protection Formula Puppy Food: Contains essential proteins, carbohydrates, and fats to support the development of strong muscles, bones, and teeth.
  • Adult Dog Food:(1-7 year)
  • Purina Pro Plan Adult Dog Food: Offers formulas for various life stages and specific needs, such as weight management or sensitive skin and stomach.
  • Taste of the Wild High Prairie Canine Formula: Grain-free option with real roasted meat for optimal amino acid and fatty acid profiles.
  • Senior Dog Food:(7+ year)
  • Nutro Ultra Senior Dry Dog Food: Formulated with lean proteins, whole grains, and a mix of superfoods to support cognitive function and maintain a healthy weight.
  • Hill's Science Diet Adult 7+ Senior Dog Food: Provides balanced nutrition with controlled levels of phosphorus and sodium to support kidney and heart health.

Key points to consider for maintaining your pet's health:

  • Regular Veterinary Care: Schedule routine check-ups with a veterinarian to monitor your pet's overall health and catch any potential issues early. Vaccinations, parasite control, dental care, and preventative measures are important components of veterinary care.
  • Nutrition: Feed your pet a balanced and appropriate diet based on their age, size, breed, and any health concerns. High-quality pet food that meets nutritional standards is essential for your pet's well-being.
  • Exercise: Provide regular exercise appropriate for your pet's age, breed, and energy level. Physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight, supports muscle tone, and promotes mental stimulation.
  • Hydration: Ensure your pet has access to clean, fresh water at all times. Proper hydration is essential for overall health and helps regulate body temperature, digestion, and organ function.
  • Grooming: Regular grooming helps keep your pet's coat and skin healthy, reduces shedding, and prevents mats and skin issues. Brushing, bathing, nail trimming, and dental care are all important aspects of grooming.
  • Emotional Well-being: Provide your pet with love, attention, and companionship. Spending quality time together strengthens the bond between you and your pet and contributes to their overall happiness and well-being.
Vaccinations for Dogs:
  • Core Vaccines:
  • Distemper
  • Parvovirus
  • Adenovirus
  • Rabies
  • Non-Core Vaccines(recommended based on individual risk factors):
  • Bordetella
  • Leptospirosis
  • Canine influenza
  • Lyme disease
Vaccinations for Cats:
  • Core Vaccines:
  • Feline viral rhinotracheitis
  • Calicivirus
  • Panleukopenia
  • Rabies
  • Non-Core Vaccines (recommended based on individual risk factors):
  • Feline leukemia virus
  • Feline immunodeficiency virus
  • Chlamydia felis
  • Bordetella
